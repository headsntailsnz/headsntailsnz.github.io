Prices<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

We have a couple of different pricing structures depending on whether or not you would like us to provide food to your pets. Royal Canin and Addiction is our choice of food for both our dogs and cats. Owners are also welcome to supply their own food if they prefer; we have fridges and freezers available to cater for raw food regimes too. You’re also able to purchase Royal Canin and Addiction pet food directly from us!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Payment is due on arrival or departure, by either cash, EFTPOS or prepaid internet banking.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
