The Basics<\/h2>\n\n\n\n
  • Payment is due on arrival or departure by Cash, Eftpos or Pre-paid by Internet Banking.<\/li>
  • Any booking request made through this website is subject to confirmation. We will email you to accept or decline your request, which is subject to availability.<\/li>
  • We charge on a 24 hour per day basis, not per night.<\/li>
  • If you drop off your dog “AM” and pick up the next day also “AM”  that is one day charge.<\/li>
  • If you drop off your dog “AM” and Pick up the next day “PM” that is one and a half day charge.<\/li>
  • We need to view a vaccination certificate, to verify your dog\/cat has been vaccinated within the last 11 months or at least 10 days before arrival. We will not accept pets for boarding unless a certificate has been previously verified.<\/li>
  • If any animal requires veterinary care \u2013 all relative costs will be met by the pet owner.<\/li>
  • We reserve the right to:
    • Make suitable arrangements for any pet left in our care 14 days after the due collecting date and if no contact is made by the owner or the owners agent; and<\/li>
    • Have any dog, which barks persistently or causes undue stress, removed by the agreed contact person. (This is always the last resort!!!); and<\/li>
    • Place dogs where we feel is appropriate to enable the smoothing running of our facility.<\/li><\/ul><\/li><\/ul>\n\n\n\n

      Holiday Period<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

      Christmas is a very busy time at Heads n Tails, you will need to make sure you book well in advance to secure a spot for your dog and\/or cat.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      A\u00a0non-refundable<\/span><\/strong>\u00a0booking fee is required to hold your booking of $50 per dog or $25 per cat. \u00a0This can be done on confirmation of booking. (Booking Fee is deducted\u00a0later from your full payment on a\u00a0day you pay the full balance).<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      You can cancel your booking and we are more than happy to refund your booking fee if you give us a minimum of 2 weeks notice so we can replace your dog with someone from our waiting list. A day or a few days doesn’t give us enough time for a replacement.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      We do not charge a surcharge on statutory days, but please note that a seven-day minimum board & time booked will be charged over the Christmas holiday period, and payment must be made for dates booked even if the animal is collected earlier. Shorter bookings over this time may be accepted too, subject to availability.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

      We are closed for pick-ups and drop-offs on Christmas and New Years Days.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
